Mass Mail

Mass E-mail is a tool for requesting and distributing official UNC-Chapel Hill messages.

This system provides support for mailing to predefined, broad sectors of the University community. Approved messages can be sent to all students, as well as to all employees. Some filtering is available to target specific subpopulations of recipients.

Things to know before creating a request:

  • Review the Mass Email Requirements for information about how the system works.
  • Details on human subject research requirements, recycling old messages, and other questions can be found in the Mass Email FAQ.
  • All requests must be approved and may be denied if there are concerns with the content or images. See the Appropriate Use section of the Mass Email Requirements for details.
  • Message approval can take 1-3 days.

To begin, choose Create Request from the menu on the left.
